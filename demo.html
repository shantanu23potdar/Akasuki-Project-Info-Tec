Welcome to PolicyGuard Guidelines

Thank you for choosing PolicyGuard, your AI-powered solution for efficient compliance monitoring and enforcement. This guide will walk you through the steps to effectively use the system and gain actionable insights for maintaining security standards. Let's get started:

Step 1: Access the Platform

Open your preferred web browser and enter the URL for PolicyGuard.

Step 2: Login or Register (If Applicable)

If you're an existing user, log in using your credentials. New users may need to register an account to access the system.

Step 3: Dashboard Overview

Upon logging in, you'll be welcomed to the PolicyGuard dashboard. Here's an overview of what you'll find:

  • Authorized and Unauthorized Actions: Visual representations of compliant and non-compliant actions with our security policies. This could include pie charts or bar graphs illustrating the distribution of compliance breaches.

Step 4: Upload Compliance Documents

Ensure accurate analysis by providing the necessary compliance documents and standards. Here's how:

  • Navigate to "Upload Compliance Documents": Look for the designated section or button.
  • Select Files: Click "Choose Files" to select your compliance documents and rule sets from your device.
  • Upload: Confirm your selection by clicking "Upload."

Step 5: Upload Logs and System Policies

To begin analysis, upload your logs and system policies. Follow these steps:

  • Navigate to "Upload Logs": Find the appropriate section.
  • Choose Files: Click "Choose Files" to select the relevant log files and system policies.
  • Upload: Confirm your selection by clicking "Upload."

Step 6: Initiate Compliance Analysis

Kickstart the analysis process to identify compliance breaches. Follow these steps:

  • Navigate to "Initiate Analysis": Locate the button to start the analysis process.
  • Start Analysis: Click "Start Analysis" to trigger the comprehensive review of logs, system policies, and compliance documents.

Step 7: Access Compliance Reports

Once analysis is complete, gain insights through detailed compliance reports. Here's how:

  • Navigate to "View Compliance Reports": Find the section to access reports.
  • Open Reports: Click on the relevant report to explore in-depth information about compliance breaches, specific lines or logs, and actionable steps to rectify issues.

Step 8: Interact with Visualizations (Optional)

Engage with interactive charts for a visual understanding of compliance status. Here's how:

  • Click on Charts: If charts such as bar graphs or pie charts are present, interact with them for detailed breakdowns and statistics.

Step 9: Log Out Securely

After utilizing PolicyGuard, ensure security by logging out of your account.

  • Navigate to "Logout": Find the "Logout" button to safely exit your account.
